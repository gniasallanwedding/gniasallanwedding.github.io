We Are Getting Married!

We are getting married on Friday, September 3rd, 2021 at Morris Arboretum in Philadelphia, PA. We hope to have all the information you will need on this website!

About Us

Laura and I first met at Drexel University in Philadelphia living in the honors dorm together. We slowly became closer friends as we realized our mutual love for books, music, gardening, and red wine.

After several years, our friendship turned into romantic interest. We had our doubts. Would we ruin our friendship? Were our families too similar? Would our relationship end after a firey debate about the best ice cream flavor (it's vanilla, @ me).

Fortunately, it worked out. We've gone on adventures, seen weird plays, eaten at every restaurant in Ardmore, raised a crazy dog, and learned how to truly understand one another. We have spent the past four years building a life toghether that will last a lifetime.

Transportation

You are welcome to arrive any time after 4PM. The ceremony will start at 6PM, so please arrive by 5:30. The venue has parking available on site, and can also accomodate Uber or Lyft rides.

Leaving there

The reception will end at 11PM, and the arboretum closes at 11:30PM. If you're using Uber or Lyft, your ride must be inside the gates before 11:30PM when the exterior gates close. The gates will open for any cars inside that are leaving after 11:30PM.

If you drove to the venue and don't feel comfortable driving home, you are permitted to leave your car overnight, so long as you pick it up before 10AM the next day. Stay safe!
