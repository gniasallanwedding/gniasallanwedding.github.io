About Us

We first met at Drexel University in Philadelphia living in the honors dorm with our friends. We became closer as we realized our mutual love for reading, music, gardening, and silliness. And after a few years we decided to go on a few dates.

It worked out. =) We have gone on adventures, seen weird plays, raised a crazy dog, and learned how to understand and take care of each other. We've spent the past five years building a love to last a lifetime.

Venue

Morris Arboretum

Both our ceremony and reception will be hosted at Morris Arboretum! We're very excited to share this beautiful place with you.

Getting there

100 E Northwestern Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19118

Guests are welcome to arrive at the arboretum at 4:00 PM or later. The ceremony will start at 6:00 PM, so please arrive by 5:30. The venue has parking available on site, and can also accomodate Uber or Lyft rides.

Heading out

The reception will end at 11:00 PM, and the arboretum closes at 11:30 PM. For guests using Uber or Lyft, your ride must be inside the gates before 11:30 PM when the exterior gates close. The gates will open for any cars inside that are leaving after 11:30 PM.

For anyone driving to the venue who would prefer not to drive home, you may leave your car overnight and pick it up before 10:00 AM the next day. Stay safe!
